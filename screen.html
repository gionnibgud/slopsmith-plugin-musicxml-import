Import MusicXML

Convert a MusicXML score into a playable sloppak with notation data and synthesized piano audio.

Requires Slopsmith with the notation format (feat/notation-format or later). Only the first part is imported. Supports dynamics, articulations, ties, grace notes, key/time signatures, and tempo changes. No repeats or da capo expansion.
